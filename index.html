LAYER: V-FAQ
What condition of scan can you handle?

Vector PDFs are the easy case. Raster scans — including older microfilm-quality sheets — go through a vision pipeline plus human check. If a sheet is genuinely unreadable, we say so instead of guessing; you don't pay for guesses.

Whose layer standard do you use?

Yours. Send a template DWG or a layer list and we match it, including text styles where practical. No standard? We ship a clean, documented default you can remap in one command.

What software does the output target?

DWG/DXF compatible with AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Carlson, MicroSurvey, and BricsCAD. Point files as PNEZD CSV. LandXML on request.

How is 48 hours possible?

Most of the redraw is automated — the same pipeline is published as open-source tooling on GitHub. The human hours go where they matter: checking courses against the record and running closure, not tracing linework.

Who's behind this?

A geomatics student and former systems engineer in British Columbia who got tired of watching skilled techs trace scans by hand. Pilot program, July 2026 — pricing and turnaround are honest because they're being measured right now.
